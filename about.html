Kerala

Kerala Tourism

One of the beautiful states in our country is Kerala. Kerala is situated in the south western region of India. A state filled with natural beauty, Kerala is also known as “God’s Own Country”. Kerala is an extraordinary state that has many attractive points inside it. The state’s traditional dances are Kathakali and Mohini Aatam. Indian elephant is considered the official animal of the state. People of Kerala speak Malayalam; and it is the official language of the state. Kerala is a tourist destination for people from all parts of the world. The most important destinations are the museum, sanctuary, temples, backwaters and beaches. Munnar, Kovalam, Kumarakom and Alappae are few places one should not miss visiting after going to Kerala.
